1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from you when you use our Service:

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data is used only to provide the requested Service.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter and submit your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
